🧬 21‑Hydroxylase Deficiency — Bold Step‑by‑Step
Step 1: Cholesterol → progesterone (shared start).

Why the pattern looks this way

  • 21‑OH (CYP21A2) block: 17‑OHP ⇏ 11‑deoxycortisol; progesterone ⇏ DOC → **↓cortisol** (± **↓aldosterone**). Excess precursors → **androgen excess**; **↑17‑OHP** is the hallmark (screening marker). (MSD; UpToDate; NEJM)
  • Phenotypes: Salt‑wasting (hyponatremia/hyperkalemia), simple virilizing, nonclassic. (UpToDate; NEJM)
🧬 11β‑Hydroxylase Deficiency — Bold Step‑by‑Step
Step 1: 17‑OHP → 11‑deoxycortisol (toward cortisol).

Why the pattern looks this way

  • 11β‑OH (CYP11B1) block: 11‑deoxycortisol & DOC accumulate; **DOC** acts as a mineralocorticoid → **HTN/hypoK**; **androgens ↑** (virilization/precocity). (MSD; Medscape)
🧬 17α‑Hydroxylase Deficiency — Bold Step‑by‑Step
Step 1: Cholesterol → progesterone (shared start).

Why the pattern looks this way

  • 17α‑OH (CYP17A1) block: fail to make 17‑OH steroids → **↓cortisol & ↓androgens**; shunt to **DOC/corticosterone ↑** → **HTN/hypoK**; delayed puberty/amenorrhea/DSD. (Medscape; MedlinePlus; J Clin Endocrinol Metab 2025; Endocrine 2024)
